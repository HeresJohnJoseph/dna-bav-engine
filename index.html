How scores work

Raw BAV scores are normalised 0–100 within your category export. A score of 72 means this brand sits at the 72nd percentile among its category peers — not an absolute measure, but a relative one. This mirrors how the Amstel deck presents DNA scores.

Connected brands drive growth
Brands are living systems, not static identities. The DNA framework treats brand equity as a dynamic — one that shifts with culture, competition, and commercial pressure. The diagnosis engine surfaces where that pressure is coming from and what to do about it.
